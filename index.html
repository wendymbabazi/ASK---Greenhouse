About Us

ASK Green House is a greenhouse farming venture in Mbarara, Uganda. We grow high-value vegetables year-round including tomatoes, onions, spinach, cabbage, green pepper, and chilli. Our goal is to provide fresh, premium-quality vegetables directly to supermarkets, restaurants, and households.

Our Services

We supply greenhouse-grown produce directly to local markets in Mbarara. By using controlled environments, we ensure that our vegetables remain fresh, consistent, and available even during dry seasons.
