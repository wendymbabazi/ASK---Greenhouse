About Us

ASK Green House is a greenhouse farming venture in Mbarara, Uganda. We grow high-value vegetables year-round including tomatoes, onions, spinach, cabbage, green pepper, and chilli. Our goal is to provide fresh, premium-quality vegetables to supermarkets, restaurants, and households.

Our Services

We supply fresh, greenhouse-grown vegetables directly to supermarkets, restaurants, and local markets in Mbarara and surrounding areas. Our controlled greenhouse ensures consistent, high-quality produce throughout the year.
